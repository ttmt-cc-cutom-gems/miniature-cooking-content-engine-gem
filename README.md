## 🧬 What is it?

The **Miniature Cooking Content Engine** is a custom Gemini Gem designed for end-to-end viral food content automation. You act as a viral media strategist and specialist in ultra-realistic "Miniature Cooking ASMR." The engine operates as a strict sequential text processing tool. With a single trigger word (`Generate`), it initiates a guided, 5-phase linear workflow that transforms a niche miniature cooking concept into a complete cross-platform content bundle—from storyboard grids to full SEO-optimized recipe articles.

---

## ⚡ The 5-Phase Pipeline

An autonomous, step-by-step workflow designed to take you from a viral concept to a multi-platform content empire.

### 1️⃣ Phase 1: Video Blueprint & Prompts
Researches current viral culinary trends and outputs a text-only blueprint matching a strict 2-clip structure (10s Prep Hook, 10s Cook & Payoff). It generates highly specific 16:9 storyboard prompts to create 4x4 continuous grid layouts of authentic miniature kitchens, paired with sequential Image-to-Video (I2V) animation prompts.

### 2️⃣ Phase 2: Social Media Captions
Outputs a complete text copy package optimized for short-form vertical video distribution across YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Facebook Reels.

### 3️⃣ Phase 3: Story & Pinterest Teaser Blueprint
Delivers a text blueprint for a high-retention 10-second vertical teaser video. Includes interactive hooks, teaser graphic prompts, I2V prompts, and dedicated Pinterest SEO fields (Title and Pin Description).

### 4️⃣ Phase 4: AdSense-Optimized Long-Form Blog Post
Provides a detailed, substantive long-form recipe article designed to satisfy Google E-E-A-T and pass AdSense quality guidelines. Features SEO metadata, historical context, precise family-portioned ingredient lists, and comprehensive step-by-step instructions.

### 5️⃣ Phase 5: Pinned Comment & Production Directives
Finalizes the bundle with optimized pinned call-to-action comments and clear production instructions for dropping the storyboard text prompts and animation guidelines into compilation tools like Google Vids.

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. Click **[Try the Gem](https://gemini.google.com/gem/10O-wx191l_AkBPg1qyiq4TLL-GChk4ns?usp=sharing)** to open it in Google Gemini.
2. Type `Generate` and hit send to initiate Phase 1.
3. Wait for the Gem to deliver the complete output for that phase and completely halt.
4. Type `Next` to seamlessly move to the subsequent phase.
5. Follow the step-by-step pipeline to generate your assets and post your viral content.
